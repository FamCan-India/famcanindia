Privacy and Ethics Disclaimer

Statement on Patient Personal Information

FamCan-India respects the confidentiality of all individuals and families whose data is included in the registry. No personally identifiable information (such as names, addresses, or contact details) is disclosed publicly. All entries are coded and anonymized to ensure privacy while preserving the integrity of clinical and genetic data.

Data Usage and Privacy Policy

Data collected in FamCan-India is used exclusively for research, clinical insight, and public health planning. Access to non-public datasets is restricted to authorized researchers and clinicians under institutional review and approval. Information is never shared with third parties for commercial purposes. Registry users must comply with strict access protocols, ensuring that sensitive data remains secure and responsibly managed.

Ethical Handling of Sensitive Data

FamCan-India adheres to the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) National Ethical Guidelines for Biomedical and Health Research Involving Human Participants (2017, updated 2023). These guidelines mandate informed consent, confidentiality, and responsible use of genetic and familial information. All registry activities are overseen by institutional ethics committees, ensuring that data collection and analysis uphold the highest standards of ethical practice in India.
