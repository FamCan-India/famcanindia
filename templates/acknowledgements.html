Acknowledgements

The FamCan-India registry owes its conception, development, and expansion to the vision, guidance, and contributions of the following individuals and institutions:

Institutional Support

Prof. Sarat Kumar Dalai – Director, Institute of Science, Nirma University; Dean, Faculty of Science

He has provided institutional oversight and academic support during FamCan-India’s initiation. A distinguished immunologist with international research experience in infectious disease and vaccine development, his leadership at ISNU ensured the registry was anchored in strong scientific governance.

Ms. Shristi Biswas – Assistant Professor, Department of Biotechnology, Silver Oak University

She is associated with Institute of Science, Nirma University as a research scholar. She coordinated the initial family registrations and contributed significantly to the development of the pilot project. Her efforts in initiating the registry from its foundation have been invaluable.

Prof. A. B. Chaudhari – Director, Krishna School of Science, Drs. Kiran and Pallavi Patel Global University; Dean, Faculty of Science

He has extended institutional backing for FamCan-India’s expansion. His role in fostering interdisciplinary collaboration at KPGU helped integrate registry development into broader academic and research frameworks.

Funding Support

Nirma University

We are thankful to the university for providing us funding of Rs. 30,00,000/- as a part of Nirma University funded Major Research Project granted to Dr. Sonal Bakshi, Institute of Science, Nirma University for her project titled "Study of constitutional genetic markers in familial cancers" in May 2022 for a period of three years.
