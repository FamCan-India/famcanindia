FamCan-India Team

Meet the team behind the successful conception, development, and deployment of the FamCan-India registry. This initiative is powered by interdisciplinary collaboration across institutions, combining scientific leadership, clinical insight, and technical expertise.

Nirma University

Dr. Sonal Rajiv Bakshi – Principal Investigator and Scientific Advisor

She conceived the idea of FamCan-India and has provided scientific leadership throughout its development. She has a Ph.D. on cytogenetics of neoplasia from Gujarat University. She is a retired Assistant Professor at Institute of Science, Nirma University and former Research Scientist at Gujarat Cancer & Research Institute, Ahmedabad. With over two decades of experience in human cytogenetics and cancer genetics, she pioneered advanced cytogenetic techniques in Gujarat and has mentored numerous researchers. Her vision transformed a research gap into a national initiative, ensuring that hereditary cancer patterns are systematically documented for the first time in India.

Dr. Darshan Dhakan – Assistant Professor, Department of Biotechnology, Institute of Science, Nirma University

He has co-ordinated registry development during its foundational phase, ensuring integration of computational and systems biology approaches. With a Ph.D. in Biological Sciences from IISER Bhopal, his expertise in computational biology, microbiome research and metagenomics has strengthened the integration of reported genetic variants in the current registry framework.

Drs. Kiran & Pallavi Patel Global University (KPGU), Vadodara

Dr. Poonam Mishra – Assistant Professor, Department of Biotechnology, Drs. Kiran & Pallavi Patel Global University

She has undertaken the task of expansion of FamCan-India, embedding interdisciplinary approaches and bioinformatics pipelines. With extensive research experience of over a decade in biotechnology and computational biology, she ensured that the registry’s pilot study evolved into a scalable, pan-India platform. A Ph.D. in Biotechnology from CSIR-IMTECH Chandigarh, her efforts and guardianship have shaped the registry in a competent form.

Hemato-Oncology Clinic (HOC Vedanta), Ahmedabad

Dr. Chirag Desai – Founder Director, Hemato-Oncology Clinic (HOC Vedanta)

He has contributed hospital-based familial cancer entries, enriching the registry’s pilot dataset with clinically verified cases. A leading medical oncologist with decades of experience, he has also served on ICMR guideline committees. His involvement ensured that FamCan-India’s data reflects real-world clinical practice and patient needs.

Kailash Cancer Hospital, Muni Seva Ashram, Goraj

Dr. Shehnaz Kantharia – ENT and Head & Neck Surgeon; Former Head of Clinical Research, Kailash Cancer Hospital and Research Centre, Muni Seva Ashram, Goraj

She provided patient entries and supported community-linked outreach. With over 20 years of experience in oncology and head & neck surgery, she played a pivotal role in developing clinical research at Kailash Cancer Hospital. Her contributions ensured that the registry captured familial cancer cases from both hospital and community networks.
